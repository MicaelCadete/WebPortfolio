SENAMUN - Worldwide Debate Simulation

Aug/2022 - dec/2022
We were part of the biggest project that our school has ever had. It was a debate simulation, basically a meeting with the leaders of each country chosen to discuss some problems around the world and try to find a resolution for them. Me and my group were representing the United States of America. We discussed two main topics, the first one was "Work in Conditions Analogous to Slavery" and the second one "The Violation of Human Rights in the Ukraine X Russia War". I made our own paperwork to help us during the debate with statistics and informations (attached below), and it was really useful... At the end of each debate we made partnerships with other countries based on our common ideas always trying to reach a final resolution. The first debate about work in conditions analogous to slavery was tied by vote, but we won the one about the violation of human rights in the Ukraine X Russia war, and the final resolution was a proposal of peace agreement made by us to stop the war. At the end of the day, me and my group were elected the best committee of our topic's debate.
